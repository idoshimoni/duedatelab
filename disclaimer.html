Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: April 2026

DueDateLab is for informational purposes only. Nothing on this site is medical advice, and no calculator on this site is a diagnostic tool.

What that means in practice

  • A due-date estimate is not a confirmed due date. Your OB or midwife confirms dating with an early ultrasound.
  • A growth percentile is a snapshot, not a report card, and a single reading cannot diagnose a growth concern. Trends over time, assessed by a pediatrician, are what matter.
  • Sleep-needs ranges are population-level guidance. Healthy children vary; edge-of-range is usually fine. Concerns about your child's sleep belong with your pediatrician.
  • The Chinese Gender Chart is a cultural novelty, not a predictor. Studies rate its accuracy at roughly that of a coin flip. Use an ultrasound or NIPT if you want a real answer.

Always consult a qualified clinician

For any decision involving your health or your child's health, speak with a licensed medical professional who knows you and your full history. Do not delay seeking or disregard advice from a clinician because of something you read or calculated on DueDateLab.

Emergencies

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in the US, 112 in the EU, 999 in the UK) or go to the nearest emergency room.
